suiCommon
directive in module sui.common

Description

Accepts common parameters like color, size, to its parent directive.

Usage

as attribute
<ANY sui-common
     color="{enum}"
     size="{enum}"
     icon="{enum}"
     disabled
     inverted="{boolean}"
     active="{boolean}"
     vertical="{boolean}">
   ...
</ANY>

Parameters

ParamTypeDetails
colorenum

red || orange || yellow || olive || green || teal || blue || violet || purple || pink || brown || grey

sizeenum

mini || tiny || small || large || huge || massive || empty

iconenum

Any https://fortawesome.github.io/Font-Awesome/icons/ icon name

disabledboolean

Whether disabled or not

invertedboolean

Inverted or not

activeboolean

Whether active or not

verticalboolean

Whether vertical or not
